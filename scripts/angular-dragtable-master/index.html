Examples of DragTable in AngularJS:

DragTable is an AngularJS directive is based upon danvk's dragtable. It does not require jQuery.

Usage:

  1. Include the dragtable module in your Angular app: var app = angular.module('demo', ['dragtable']);
  2. Add the draggable attribute to any table. Eg: 
                    <table draggable>
                ...
                </table>
